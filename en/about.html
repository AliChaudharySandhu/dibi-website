About the Project

Decarbonisation Pathways for Indonesia’s Buses Infrastructure (DIBI).

Overview

This site shares research, case studies, and inclusive practices to support bus electrification in Indonesia.

Objectives

  • Provide a bilingual knowledge hub (English & Indonesian).
  • Highlight energy, air quality, and depot planning insights.
  • Emphasize GEDSI (Gender Equality, Disability, Social Inclusion).

Partners & Audience

Built for transport planners, researchers, government agencies, and the wider community.
